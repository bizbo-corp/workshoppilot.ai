## Unsatisfied Requirements Detail

### PRIC-01: Pricing page displays updated tiers

**Required:** Single Flight ($79), Serial Entrepreneur ($149), Agency (Contact Sales) — three tiers
**Actual:** "Single Pilot" ($99), "Serial Entrepreneur" ($199) — two tiers, wrong name, wrong prices
**Evidence:** `src/app/pricing/page.tsx` getTiers() returns exactly 2 items; grid is `md:grid-cols-2`
**Fix:** Update tier name to "Single Flight", update prices to $79/$149, add Agency tier, change grid to 3-column

### PRIC-02: Agency tier shows Contact Sales

**Required:** Agency tier with "Contact Sales" button (no self-serve billing)
**Actual:** No Agency tier exists anywhere in pricing page
**Evidence:** `getTiers()` returns array with no Agency entry; no mailto or contact form link in file
**Fix:** Add third tier to getTiers() with no priceId, "Contact Sales" CTA linking to mailto or contact form

### PRIC-03: Self-serve tiers link to Stripe Checkout (PARTIAL)

**Required:** Single Flight and Serial Entrepreneur link to Stripe Checkout
**Actual:** Both existing tiers have `form method="POST" action="/api/billing/checkout"` — mechanically wired
**Issue:** Price copy ($99/$199) doesn't match Stripe charge amount ($79/$149). User sees $99, gets charged $79.
**Fix:** Align displayed prices with Stripe Product prices
