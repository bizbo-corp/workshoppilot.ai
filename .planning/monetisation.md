Recommended Approach: The "Modified Pay-for-Results" (Hybrid)

Given your $10 token cost, Option 3 is dangerous (you'll lose money on every single-use customer), and Option 1 is a hard sell for a new tool.

I recommend a "Taste Test" Paywall. Here is how it works:

The Hook (Free): Let them run the first 2-3 steps of the Design Thinking process (e.g., Problem Definition and User Personas). This costs you cents, not dollars.

The Micro-Paywall: To proceed into the "Deep Work" (where the $10 token spend happens), they must purchase a Workshop Pass.

The Deliverable: The "Build Pack" is included in that pass.
